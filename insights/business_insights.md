# Business Insights

## Overview
This analysis was performed on the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset using PostgreSQL.

The project focused on:
- revenue analysis
- customer behavior
- product performance
- sales trends

---

# Revenue Insights

- Certain product categories generated significantly higher revenue than others.
- Revenue concentration was heavily skewed toward a few high-performing categories.
- Monthly revenue trends showed fluctuations across different periods.

---

# Customer Insights

- A small percentage of customers contributed disproportionately high spending.
- Repeat purchasing behavior existed among top-ranked customers.
- Customer activity varied significantly across regions.

---

# Product Insights

- Some categories had high order frequency but lower overall revenue.
- Higher-priced categories contributed more revenue despite fewer purchases.
- Product category distribution was uneven across total sales.

---

# Operational Insights

- Freight costs varied considerably across product categories.
- Order activity changed over time, indicating seasonality patterns.
- Transaction volume increased during specific periods.
